My Recipe of Butter Pav-Bhaji

Ingredients

  • pav
  • 4- boiled Pottatoes
  • 6-Tomatoes
  • 4-Onions
  • 2-Green Chillies
  • 1-tablespoon ginger garlic paste
  • 4-french beans
  • 3-Capsicum
  • !00gm- Green peas
  • Pav Bhaji Masala
  • Salt
  • 300grms- Butter
  • Coriander

Instructions for the Preparation of Butter Pav Bhaji

  1. Heat a pan, add 1 tsp oil, 1/4 tsp jeera, 1/2 tsp ginger garlic paste, 1/4 cup chopped onion and saute it well.
  2. Then add 1/2 tsp salt, 1/4 cup chopped bottle gourd, 1/4 cup boiled peas, 1/4 cup chopped cauliflower, water as required, 1/2 tsp haldi, saute them well.
  3. Then add 1/4 cup boiled and diced potato, saute well. Add water as required and cover with lid & cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Remove the lid, mash the bhaji with a masher.
  5. Add 1/2 cup chopped tomato,1 tbsp pav bhaji masala, 1 tbsp red chilli powder, 1 tsp dhania powder, 1/4 tsp amchur powder,1 tsp butter, mix and mash well.
  6. Then Add 2 tbsp chopped capsicum, mix and mash it well. Cover with lid, cook for 5 minutes. Mash them well again.
  7. Garnish it with 1 tbsp chopped coriander leaves, mix it well.
  8. Serve it hot with pav and chopped onions.
